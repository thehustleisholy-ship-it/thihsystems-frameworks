Research register, claims ledger, deterministic synthetic illustration, open rules, privacy boundaries, publication package, and release gate.

## Next, if independently continued

Lived-experience review; authorized-practitioner review; interpreter/transliterator review; threat modeling; low-literacy and assistive-technology testing; translated interface research.

Personal-data intake, narrative generation, legal analysis, government integration, production deployment, institutional adoption, regulatory approval, or field pilot.
