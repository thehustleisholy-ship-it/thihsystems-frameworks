# Privacy architecture

## Principles

Collect less; separate identity from working records; preserve unchanged originals; encrypt; make access visible; obtain specific revocable consent; minimize third-party data; expire access; delete verifiably; assume devices and accounts may be monitored.

## Threats requiring design review

Device seizure or shared-device access, abusive account control, phishing, unsafe downloads, metadata leakage, witness exposure, cross-border access, insider access, model retention, logs/backups that defeat deletion, and mistaken recipient selection.

## Public demo rule

No text fields, uploads, persistence, analytics tied to a person, network submission, or real scenarios. Presets are synthetic. Export is a visible readiness control only and does not produce a file.
