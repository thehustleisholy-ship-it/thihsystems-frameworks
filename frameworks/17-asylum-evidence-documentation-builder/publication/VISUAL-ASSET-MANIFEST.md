# Visual asset production manifest

Global direction: humane editorial abstraction—layered paper edges, timeline nodes, language/provenance tags, open gaps, and protective containers. Obsidian, warm ivory, muted mint, amber. Never use border fences, detention imagery, passports as identity shorthand, faceless crowds, crying-child closeups, flags, handcuffs, surveillance aesthetics, or graphic/sensational scenes. Every asset footer: **Proposed framework · Synthetic educational illustration · Not legal advice**. Legal/process facts use source keys from `research/SOURCE-REGISTER.md`; place source line in caption or final card, never imply a cited agency endorses the framework.

## Export requirements

PNG plus editable source for each; sRGB; text contrast ≥4.5:1 except large text ≥3:1; disclaimer readable at rendered size; no baked-in URLs too small to read; alt text travels with upload; retain a source/copy approval record. Screenshot paths are fixed in `release-manifest.json`.
