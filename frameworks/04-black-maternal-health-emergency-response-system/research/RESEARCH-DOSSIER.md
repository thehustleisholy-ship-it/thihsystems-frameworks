# Framework 04 Research Dossier

Reviewed 2026-07-16. This is a primary-source metadata review, not clinical, legal, privacy, or expert approval.

## Verified burden

- FW-04-A-001: 2024 NVSS maternal mortality was 17.9 deaths per 100,000 live births (649 deaths). NVSS uses a maternal-death definition generally limited to pregnancy through 42 days.
- FW-04-A-002: 2024 NVSS rates were 44.8 for non-Hispanic Black women and 14.2 for non-Hispanic White women per 100,000 live births; the observed year-over-year changes were not statistically significant.
- FW-04-A-003: CDC reports more than 80% of pregnancy-related deaths reviewed by MMRCs in 36 states for 2017–2019 were determined preventable. This is not a national mortality rate.
- FW-04-A-004: NVSS, PMSS, and MMRC outputs are not directly interchangeable.
- FW-04-A-005: CDC Hear Her provides warning-sign education during pregnancy and through one year after delivery and directs people with listed signs to immediate medical care.

## Qualified regulatory propositions

FW-04-A-006 through A-009 establish only conditional boundaries for HIPAA, FTC breach notification, FDA software oversight, and evidence authentication. Product-specific conclusions require qualified counsel and, for FDA scope, intended-use review.

## Unresolved queue

Clinical dismissal and pain bias; leading causes and postpartum timing beyond the approved definitions above; early-warning effectiveness; patient-activated rapid response; competitor capability; doula outcomes; and individual cases remain unapproved pending focused review. No Class F claim exists.

## Definition firewall

Maternal mortality (NVSS, generally 42 days), pregnancy-related mortality (PMSS, one year and causal relationship), pregnancy-associated death (temporal relationship through one year), severe maternal morbidity, and MMRC preventability findings must always be labeled separately.
