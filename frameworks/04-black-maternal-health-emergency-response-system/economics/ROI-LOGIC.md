# ROI Logic

No ROI is verified or approved. No avoided-death value, outcome improvement, or adverse-event reduction may be claimed.

A future model may inventory implementation costs, documentation time, response staffing, training, privacy/security controls, and evaluation costs. Any benefit scenario must identify assumptions and remain Class E until measured. Actual effects require governed pilot evidence and would become Class F only after review.

Open inputs: accountable operator, workflow, response capacity, baseline, comparison design, outcome definitions, time horizon, implementation cost, and unintended-harm measures.
