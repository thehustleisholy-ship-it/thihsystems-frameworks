# Pilot Guide

Status: not pilot-ready. No partner, recruitment, approval, or outcome exists.

Before a pilot: name an accountable clinical operator; complete IRB/ethics determination where applicable; complete legal/privacy/FDA/security/accessibility reviews; define emergency ownership; test failure modes with synthetic data; establish consent, withdrawal, deletion, and adverse-event processes.

A future feasibility pilot could measure usability, documentation completeness, escalation-log delivery, response-process reliability, false reassurance/alarm risks, burden, privacy incidents, and equitable accessibility. It must not use mortality reduction as an early feasibility endpoint or imply clinical efficacy.

Stop criteria include delayed care, unclear emergency messaging, unowned escalations, privacy/security incident, discriminatory performance, inaccessible critical flows, or participant distress without support.
