# Framework Friday 04 — Draft Publication Package

Status: draft; Structured Framework; not clinically validated, expert-reviewed, pilot-ready, or field-validated.

## Central thesis

Maternal concerns, warning-sign education, escalation attempts, and responses should be easier to document without turning a documentation tool into a diagnostic system.

## Verified context

Use only FW-04-A-001, A-002, and A-003 with their complete dates and definitions. NVSS 42-day maternal mortality and MMRC one-year pregnancy-related preventability findings are different measures.

## What is proposed

Patient-controlled documentation, source-linked generic education, advocate/escalation logging, explicit emergency-call initiation, and provenance-aware export.

## What is not claimed

THIHsystems has not solved maternal mortality. There is no clinical validation, partner, adoption, expert approval, pilot, ROI, legal conclusion, or measured outcome.

## Current build status

The research and safety package is documented; the interactive remains a concept preview. Unresolved evidence stays visible.
