# Policy Brief

## Problem

Current primary sources document severe racial disparity in NVSS maternal mortality and substantial preventability findings among reviewed pregnancy-related deaths, but those statistics use different definitions and must not be merged.

## Proposal

Explore patient-controlled documentation, generic warning-sign education, explicit escalation logging, and accountable response workflows as infrastructure hypotheses. Policy work should focus on listening, response ownership, privacy, correction rights, accessibility, and transparent failure reporting.

## Not claimed

This framework has not reduced mortality, been clinically validated, been adopted by an institution, or established a legal right to admission of generated records. It does not replace emergency care or clinical judgment.

## Required review

Obstetric safety, emergency operations, health equity, disability access, privacy/security, FDA scope, state law, evidence law, ethics, and patient/community governance.
