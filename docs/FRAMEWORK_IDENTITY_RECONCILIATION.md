# Framework Identity Reconciliation

## Decision

`src/lib/framework-master-matrix.ts` is the sole active authority for framework
number, title, slug, routes, maturity, evidence status, and relationships.
`framework-content.ts` retains editorial copy but derives identity and routes.

## Conflicts found

The 2026-07-09 interactive artifact plan represented an earlier portfolio.
Frameworks 04, 06–09, and 11–30 contained titles or slugs that did not match the
active catalog. Framework 05 differed only in title wording. The original
records remain in `archivedInteractiveArtifactRecords` for historical value;
they cannot be treated as active numbered frameworks.

The Service Trade Intake Stewardship and Real Estate Client Clarity directories
predate the current catalog. They are preserved as legacy archive material and
are not active Frameworks 02 or 03. Active Framework 02 is Job-Loss &
Income-Shock Stabilizer; active Framework 03 is Returning Citizen Reentry
Roadmap. Framework 04 remains Black Maternal Health Emergency Response System.

## Unresolved

Archived concepts have not been evaluated for future unnumbered publication.
No archived concept will be renumbered or reactivated without a separate,
documented catalog decision.
